About BioCommons Ghana
Who we are, why we exist, and how the platform works.

BioCommons Ghana is a free, open hub that connects biological scientists across Ghana to the opportunities that move careers and research forward — scholarships, fellowships, grants, jobs, reagent exchange, workshops, and graduate programmes, all in one place.

Too much of this information is scattered across mailing lists, WhatsApp groups, and notice boards — and too often it reaches people only after the deadline has passed. We bring it together, verify the links, surface what's closing soon, and keep it open to everyone. No paywalls, no accounts required, no gatekeeping.

Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 2026

BioCommons Ghana is a free community resource that connects biological scientists in Ghana to scholarships, grants, jobs, reagents, events, and programmes. This notice explains, in plain language, what information we handle and why. We try to collect as little as possible.

Analytics (only with your consent)

We use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used — for example, which sections are most visited — so we can improve it. No analytics cookies are placed unless you click “Accept” on the cookie banner. If you accept, Google Analytics may collect your approximate location, the pages you view, how you arrived at the site, and basic device and browser information. We have enabled IP anonymisation. If you click “Reject,” analytics never loads and no analytics cookies are set.

Information stored only in your browser

When you bookmark opportunities to your “My List,” and when you record your cookie choice, that information is saved locally in your own browser. It never leaves your device and is not sent to us. Clearing your browser data removes it.

Information you choose to give us

Some features ask for details directly:

  • Newsletter: if you subscribe, we collect your name, institution, and email so we can send the monthly newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Deadline reminders: if you ask to be reminded about an opportunity, we store your email only to send that reminder.
  • Submitting a listing: if you submit a scholarship, grant, job, reagent, event, or programme, we receive the details you provide. Every submission is reviewed by our team before it goes live.

This information is sent to and stored in our backend (a Google Workspace service) and is used only to run BioCommons Ghana.

What we don’t do

We don’t sell your information, we don’t show ads, and we don’t share your details with third parties for marketing.

Third-party services

We rely on Google services (Google Analytics for usage statistics, and Google Workspace for storing submissions and signups). Their handling of data is governed by Google’s own privacy policy.

Your choices & contact

You can change or withdraw your analytics consent at any time by clearing this site’s data in your browser, which makes the cookie banner appear again. For any privacy question, or to ask us to remove your details, email us at biocommonsghana@gmail.com and we’ll respond within 24 hours.

🍪 Cookies on BioCommons Ghana

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. No analytics cookies are set unless you accept. See our privacy notice for details.
